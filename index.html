Wind model: a diagnostic, mass-conserving flow approximation (not full CFD). It captures deflection, ridge speed-up, orographic lift/sink and an empirical lee rotor — useful for planning, but real low-level flow is more complex. Treat rotor/lift as guidance, not guarantees.

Safety / legal: Airspace data here is approximate and for planning only. 92057 borders Camp Pendleton (military, no overflight), KOKB and KCRQ airports. Before flying, always confirm with the FAA B4UFLY app, LAANC, and current NOTAMs. Recreational flyers must stay ≤400 ft AGL, within line of sight, and follow the FAA Recreational rules & a CBO safety code. I'm not a legal authority — verify before you fly.
